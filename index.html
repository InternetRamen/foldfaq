Banano Folding FAQ

Q: Where do I start mining?

Get your folding username from https://bananominer.com, then download the F@H client from https://foldingathome.org/start-folding/

Q: I haven't got paid yet, when will I be paid?

First payment is normally within 24-36 hours of completing first Work Unit (green bar goes to 100%) as long as you complete at least 2 work units across two 12 hour periods.

You can validate your configuration and check your stats here - https://monkey2monkey.com/fh-stats/

Q: Can I use anything as my name?

No. Enter your Banano address into https://bananominer.com/ and it will provide you with a name and a team number. Enter these into the Folding@Home config panel. If you don't have these set correctly, you will not be paid. Copy and paste to ensure it's correct.

Q: How many bananos per day can I get from [Insert graphics card here]

Use this site to search for the PPD (points per day) of your graphics card: https://folding.lar.systems/gpu_ppd/overall_ranks
Then, input the PPD into this calculator: https://turtlebyte.github.io/bananocalculator/

Q: How do I remove my GPU/CPU from folding?

1. Click on the "Configure" button in the top left of the F@HControl client software.
2. Go on the "Slots" tab and add/remove whichever folding slot you want to.
3. Click Save

Q: Why am I only getting the Base Credit for my completed Work Units?

Your Quick Return Bonus (Passkey Bonus) is not active. A Quick return bonus requires 80% successful completion rate of Work Units, and 10 Work Units completed after entering passkey into client. You can get a passkey here: https://apps.foldingathome.org/getpasskey

Q: My GPU is only reporting 10-30% utlization in Task Manager?

Task manager misreports GPU utilization for Folding@Home. Change to CUDA or Compute depending on Nvidia or AMD GPU or use HWinfo/alternative monitoring software.

Q: Is CPU folding even worth it?

Most people only fold with their GPU as CPU folding is really inefficient with lower end CPUs. If you have a good CPU and you are fine with the thermals, feel free to turn it on.
